Real Time Tracking

Track the location of your objects (vehicles, people, phones, bikes, animals) online in real-time. View additional information about the tracking objects, including its speed, exact address, fuel consumption and travel history.

Mobile Application

Download one of our mobile GPS tracking apps.Transform your mobile (iPhone and Android) into a GPS tracker and track it online in real time. Or choose the TABATOO mobile client app and access all the software features on your Smartphone or Tablet.

Vehicle Performance

Easily check the tank’s fuel level and fuel consumption along the route in real time. Check for driving style that can directly influence fuel consumption. It is a fact that improving driving behavior can cut your fuel costs significantly (by 5% to 15%).

Geofencing

The Geofence feature allows you to set up geographic boundaries around areas that have specific interest for you. Receive automated alerts when the object enters or leaves marked boundaries.

Notifications

Get instant alerts about your tracking object. Know when the object enters or exits a geo-zone. Get SOS alarms if your car has been stolen, and easily turn the engine on or off. Get notifications to your iPhone or Android devices, via e-mail, mobile app or SMS.

Cloud GPS Tracking Software

TABATOO GPS Software can monitor thousands of targets, and display their location on maps with crystal clear detail right down to street level in a web browser.
All made possible only with the power of cloud and GPS tracker.

Software is very simple, user friendly and designed to communicate with a wide variety of devices including mobile devices. It is constantly improved and updated with new features.
