OUR STORY

TABATOO GPS SOLUTIONS is high quality tracking and fleet management software developed by a team of professional software and hardware engineers. As one of the leading developers of GPS tracking and fleet management software solutions in Mauritania, TABATOO GPS SOLUTIONS software was developed in 2019 by a team of professional engineers. The company was founded by the current CEO Fousseynou COULIBALY.

TABATOO GPS SOLUTIONS has already positioned itself as the Mauritanian leading GPS tracking software firm and is already linked to the implementation of GPS trackers for many of the Mauritanian Businesses.
