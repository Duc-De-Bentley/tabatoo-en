OUR STORY

TABATOO GPS SOLUTIONS is high quality tracking and fleet management software developed by a team of professional software and hardware Mauritanian Engineers. The company is one of the leading developers of GPS tracking and fleet management software solutions in Mauritania. 

TABATOO GPS SOLUTIONS software was developed in 2019 by a team of professional and passionate Engineers amongst them Fousseynou COULIBALY who is the current CEO and the company founder.

The name TABATOO is inspired by the Soninke language, a language commonly spoken in Mauritania. TABATOO literally means "footprints".

TABATOO GPS SOLUTIONS has already positioned itself as the Mauritanian leading GPS tracking solutions firm and is already linked to the implementation of GPS trackers for many of the Mauritanian Businesses.
